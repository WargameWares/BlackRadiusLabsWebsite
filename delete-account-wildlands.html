Delete Your Wildlands Account

In-App Deletion (Recommended)

You can delete your Wildlands account directly in the app from:

Settings → Account → Delete Account

This is the fastest way to permanently remove your account and associated game data.

Manual Deletion Request

If you cannot access the app and need to request deletion manually, please contact:

admin@blackradiuslabs.com

Please include:

  • Your Player ID (found in Settings → Account in the game)
  • Your Google Play Games username (if your account is linked)
  • Any additional details that may help us identify your account

We may contact you to verify ownership before processing the request.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, we will remove:

  • Your authentication/account record
  • Your cloud save and game progress
  • Associated stored gameplay data linked to your account

Important Information

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Some non-identifiable aggregated analytics data may be retained.
  • Certain data may be retained where required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.
  • Manual deletion requests are typically processed within 7 days.
